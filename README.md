# Welcome

### The World's First White-Label Prediction Market Engine

Welcome to the official documentation of PrometheX. PrometheX is the premier Prediction Market as a Service (PMaaS) infrastructure provider, offering a comprehensive engine for third parties to deploy and operate their own prediction markets.

### Our Vision

PrometheX is designed to provide prediction market infrastructure for everyone. We act as the "enabler" in the industry—the "Shopify" for prediction markets—empowering you to build your own brand rather than competing for your users.

### Why Prediction Markets?

Prediction markets are currently the fastest-growing financial derivative class. With the industry volume reaching over $44 billion in 2025 and monthly active users climbing past 600,000, the opportunity for growth is unprecedented. PrometheX provides the technical foundation for you to capture this momentum ahead of major global catalysts like the 2026 World Cup.

### The PrometheX Advantage

* 100% White-Label Ownership: Deployed under YOUR Brand and on your custom domain. Unlike competitors, we ensure you maintain full ownership of your user base and data.
* Proprietary APMM Engine: Our Automated Prediction Market Maker (APMM) ensures deep markets and instant liquidity from day one. There is no "chicken and egg" problem; the smart contract is always ready to buy or sell.
* Non-Custodial & Trustless: PrometheX is non-custodial by design. All trades and settlements occur via trustless, on-chain smart contracts. Funds are held in audited contracts, never by us or the partner.
* Rapid Time-to-Market: While building from scratch can take 12–18 months, PrometheX allows you to be live and trading in just 4 weeks.

### Platform Scope

PrometheX supports 13 Market Categories, providing comprehensive coverage from macro events to niche interests:

* Financial & Tech: Crypto & DeFi (Price feeds, TVL, Gas), Economics, and AI & Tech.
* Sports & Culture: Match outcomes, Player stats, Esports, and Pop Culture.
* Global Affairs: Politics (Elections, Bills, Policy) and Geopolitics.
* Future Frontiers: Climate, Health, Science, and Space.
